About Om Nom Run

Om Nom Run is an exciting endless runner game where you control the adorable Om Nom character through various challenging levels. Jump over obstacles, collect candies, and power-ups while trying to achieve the highest score. With its colorful graphics, smooth controls, and addictive gameplay, this free online game provides endless fun for players of all ages.

Game Features

  • Simple and intuitive controls
  • Endless gameplay with increasing difficulty
  • Collect candies and power-ups
  • Multiple obstacles and challenges
  • Free to play online
