About Om Nom Run

Om Nom Run is an addictive endless runner game where you control the cute character Om Nom. Jump over obstacles, slide under barriers, and collect delicious candies along the way. The game features smooth controls and increasing difficulty to keep you engaged!

How to Play

  • Use the spacebar or tap to jump
  • Press down arrow or swipe down to slide
  • Collect candies to increase your score
  • Avoid obstacles to stay alive
  • Try to achieve the highest score possible!
