About Om Nom Run

Om Nom Run is an engaging endless runner game that brings the beloved character Om Nom to life in an exciting adventure. Navigate through various obstacles, collect candies, and try to achieve the highest score possible. With smooth controls and addictive gameplay, this free online game offers endless entertainment for players of all ages.

Game Features

  • Simple and intuitive controls
  • Endless gameplay with increasing difficulty
  • Collect candies and power-ups
  • Multiple obstacles and challenges
  • Free to play online
