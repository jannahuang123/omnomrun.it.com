About Om Nom Run

Om Nom Run is an exciting endless runner game featuring the lovable character Om Nom from the popular Cut the Rope series. In this fast-paced adventure, you'll help Om Nom navigate through colorful environments, collecting sweet candies while avoiding various obstacles.

With simple controls and addictive gameplay, Om Nom Run is perfect for players of all ages. The vibrant graphics and charming character designs make this game a delightful experience for everyone.

Game Features

  • Simple one-touch controls
  • Colorful and vibrant environments
  • Collect candies and power-ups
  • Avoid obstacles and enemies
  • Unlock special abilities and characters
  • Challenge yourself with increasing difficulty
  • Free to play online anytime

How to Play

Controls: Use your mouse, touchscreen, or keyboard to help Om Nom jump, slide, and move through the course.

Objective: Collect as many candies as possible while avoiding obstacles. The further you run, the higher your score!

Tips: Watch out for special power-ups that can give Om Nom special abilities like magnets for candies or temporary invincibility.
